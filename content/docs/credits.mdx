## Upstream projects

I built Unlumen UI on open-source work from [Animate UI](https://animate-ui.com), [shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com), [Motion](https://motion.dev), [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com), [Lucide](https://lucide.dev), and [Fumadocs](https://fumadocs.vercel.app).

Some registry structure and animation patterns originated in Animate UI. I preserve the applicable upstream license notices and attribute the projects above. Before contributing copied or adapted material, verify that its license permits redistribution under this repository's MIT terms.
