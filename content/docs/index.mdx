**Unlumen UI** is a collection of re-usable components that you can copy and paste into your web apps. Built with Tailwind CSS and Motion, based on [shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com/) patterns, and forked from [animate UI](https://animate-ui.com).

# Whoami

I love building things, and I started to find happiness sharing to others.

I'm Léo 👋, an independent designer and developer, working on Unlumen while studying computer science. Then I started building Unlumen UI as a way to share what I learned about polished interfaces.

## Philosophy

I believe design is one of the first signals a product sends.

People don’t start with your features. They start with a feeling. In seconds, they ask themselves simple things

- Is this legit
- Can I trust this
- Was this built with care

Design answers those questions before anything else.

When something feels generic or unfinished, it creates distance; when every detail feels intentional, it does the opposite. It shows respect; it builds trust; it makes people want to stay.

I remember discovering Vercel and being blown away by how polished everything felt. The animations, the colors, the typography; everything worked together to create an experience that felt premium. It made me want to use their product before I even understood what it did.

That’s why I built Unlumen UI.

To help create interfaces that feel intentional; not just nicer to look at, but better to experience.

Hope you'll find something useful in here. And if you do, I’d love to see what you build with it on [x.com/leouiux](https://x.com/leouiux).
